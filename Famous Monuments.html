Eiffel Tower

Initially built to serve as the entrance gateway to the International Exposition of 1889 as well as a testament to French industrial ingenuity.
Taj Mahal

An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife.
Cn Tower

The CN Tower was finished on April 2, 1975, opening to the public on June 26, 1976. At 553 metres (1,815 feet), it would remain the tallest freestanding structure in the world until surpassed by Dubai's Burj Khalifa in 2009.
Statue Of Liberty

She is an icon, a national treasure, and one of the most recognizable figures in the world.
